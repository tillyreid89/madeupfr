Situated right on the Mediterranean Sea in Beaulieu-sur-Mer, sits the idyllic Villa Kerylos. Built in the early 20th Century, the greek inspired property is an architects dream and the perfect hide away for those wanting to escape the bright lights of Nice and Monaco. From intricately hand-painted ceilings, to beautifully designed mosaic flooring, the interiors of this building are truly stunning. Whatever the time of year, this is somewhere you’ll undoubtedly feel inspired.
